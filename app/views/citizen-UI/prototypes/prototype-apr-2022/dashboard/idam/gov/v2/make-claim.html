Make a money claim online

You can make a money claim if you believe a person or organisation owes you money and won’t pay you back. You'll have to pay a fee.

Before making a claim, contact the person or organisation to try to resolve the issue by discussion or mediation.

Your claim, including your name and address, will be sent to the person you say owes you money. They'll get a chance to respond to your claim.
