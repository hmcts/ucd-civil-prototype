Respond to a money claim

You must respond if someone claims you owe them money. The deadline for responding is on the email or letter you received explaning the claim.

If you don't respond

You might get a County Court Judgment (CCJ) (opens in a new window) if you don’t respond by the deadline.

If you get a CCJ:

  • bailiffs might be sent to your home or business
  • you might have problems getting credit, such as a mobile phone contract, credit card or mortgage
