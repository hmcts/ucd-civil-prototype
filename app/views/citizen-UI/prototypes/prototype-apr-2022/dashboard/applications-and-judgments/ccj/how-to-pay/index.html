How to pay a County Court Judgment (CCJ)

Contact the claimant and agree how to pay. For example by cheque or bank transfer. You can't pay the court.

Make sure you can prove you’ve paid. Get a receipt from the claimant if you pay by cheque or cash directly. Use the claim number on all payments.

Keep a record of all payments

Allow up to 4 days for your payment to reach the claimant.

If you can't afford to pay

You can suggest your own repayment plan. This is known as 'vary an order'.

To vary an order costs £50
