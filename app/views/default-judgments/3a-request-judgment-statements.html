When can I request default judgment?

You can only request default judgment if:

  • the time for responding to the claim has expired
  • the Defendant has not responded to the claim
  • there is no outstanding application by the Defendant to strike out the claim or for summary judgment
  • the Defendant has not satisfied the whole claim, including costs
  • the Defendant has not filed an admission together with request for time to pay

You can change your answer or wait until all of the statements apply to the Defendant to request default judgment.
