Apply to cancel (set aside) the CCJ

You can apply to get the CCJ 'set aside'. You'll have to pay a court fee of £255. If you're on a low income you can get help with fees.

You'll need to show that you responded to the claim. You may need to go to a hearing.

If your application is successful

Your CCJ will be removed from public records. The court will restart the claim.

You can request the claimant refund your £255 fee.

If your application is unsuccessful

You might have to pay the claimant's costs. For example their legal fees or travel costs if it goes to a hearing.

The CCJ will stay on the register for 6 years.
